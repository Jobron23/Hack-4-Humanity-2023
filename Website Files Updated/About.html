How It Works

Input Location

Our alorithms take your location and use water safety APIs to evaluate the cleanliness of your drinking water

Water Readings

The water readings based on your location return pH levels, and other particle contamination

Safety

Based on these readings, we evaluate whether the water is safe for drinking

The Creators

During the 2023 Hack 4 Humanity event at Santa Clara University, we created Project Safe Water with the hope of providing more people with the ability to evaluate local water sources and provide more information to those in need. With recent attention to providing safe drinking water to more areas of the world, we created the project with that in mind.
